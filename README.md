# You want to control your Airship with the Neiry handband?
Now you CAN!!

We made this at the event by Central University at STEM School in Moscow.

# Instructions
Download the main script and Capsule SDK (I hate this so much) and just start!
1. Connect to your headband
2. Think about turning left and press the button
3. The AI will learn your movements
After 10 training presses, the AI starts decoding your EEG activity from the headband in real time.
After learning, you can connect to your airship and control it with your brain.
